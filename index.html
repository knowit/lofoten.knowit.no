Talks and Speakers

A farewell to JavaScript? Looking at Scala.js

Scala.js compiles your Scala code to JavaScript, allowing you to write your entire web-application in Scala (with a bit of HTML and CSS, of course). This talk gives an introduction to Scala.js. You will see examples of developer tools, roundtrip, unit testing and sharing of code between front- and backend. Version 0.6.0 of Scala.js was released in February 2015, and was the first to be declared "production ready". Any code you write now will work throughout the 0.6.x and 1.x versions. In other words, this is the perfect time to get in on the ground floor!
